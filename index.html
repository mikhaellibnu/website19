About me

Hello, I'm Mikhaellibnu. I am a Digital Business undergraduate with a strong interdisciplinary background in design, digital media, and creative strategy. My academic journey, combined with hands-on creative work, has equipped me with versatile skills in music production, video editing, and visual communication. This blend of technical capability and artistic insight enables me to approach every project with creativity, precision, and a clear sense of purpose.

I am dedicated to continuous learning and professional growth. Exploring modern tools, industry trends, and emerging creative techniques allows me to stay adaptable in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. Whether working independently or as part of a team, I strive to deliver work that is polished, thoughtful, and impactful. My goal is to contribute creative solutions that inspire, resonate, and leave a lasting impression.
